# Universal Travels – Project Case Study

## Project Overview

Universal Travels is an established travel agency providing Umrah travel packages, international travel planning, flight bookings, holiday packages, and travel support services.

The project involved developing a modern travel website that would improve customer engagement, provide service visibility, and simplify inquiry generation through digital channels.

Website:
https://www.unitravels.co.za/

---

## Business Challenge

The company required a website capable of:

- Presenting travel services professionally
- Promoting Umrah packages
- Increasing customer inquiries
- Improving online visibility
- Supporting mobile users
- Establishing trust and credibility

---

## Solution

A responsive travel agency website was developed featuring:

- Travel package presentation
- Service-focused content pages
- Inquiry management functionality
- Responsive user interfaces
- Search engine optimization
- Mobile-friendly navigation

The platform serves as a digital gateway for customers seeking travel services and Umrah package information.
